General awareness

Knowledge is power. Without being aware of general hygiene practices and protocols to stay healthy, we can't truly protect ourselves from the virus. Not all of us can volunteer to do social service, but the utmost social service that one can do is to follow these practices and stay fit. Remember, when a single person gets infected by the virus all the people around him/her are also endangered. Maintaining your health is a social responsiblity.
