fightcorona is an NGO aimed at providing aid to the poor sections of the society that have been affected by the coronavirus pandemic as well as its effects which include unemployment and scarcity of daily commodities. Started on 10th March 2020 by a socially aware group of college students, today we are a team of over 300+ volunteers. The NGO is currently based in the city of Pune but we plan to expand our reach to nearby towns and villages as well.

How can you help?

As the daily tally of coronavirus cases keeps on increasing, the NGO requires many more more hands to come together. Join us as a volunteer to help those in need. The real corona warriors may be the doctors, nurses and health care professionals relentlessly working to save lives and the police patrolling the streets but we as responsible citizens should also take responsibility to fight the crisis. Instead of watching from the sidelines, join us on the frontlines.
