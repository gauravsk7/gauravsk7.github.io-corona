The coronavirus is a real threat to humanity. Global leaders may try to downplay its threat to prevent panic, but there are no two ways about it. This is the biggest crisis that this generation has seen.It continues to affect people around the globe with an alarming rate of growth.


Although the government of India has imposed stringent lockdowns to curb the spread of the virus, it continues to affect the poorest sections of the society. Industries have been shut down and this has affected daily wage earners as they have no way to earn their bread anymore. In many cases people who had gone to other states in search of jobs have been left stranded away from their homes. For them, the virus is not the only threat - they also have to face hunger and starvation. We here at fightcorona believe that it is our moral duty to help each other in times of crisis.
